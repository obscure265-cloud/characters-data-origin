THE HAUNTING STALKER

The Haunting Stalker (Venatophasma) is a rare paranormal entity studied by AERD and classified as a Paramorph Type-A/B, appearing largely human but with subtle anatomical abnormalities optimized for speed and predatory movement. They inhabit isolated locations such as forests, caves, and abandoned structures, where they silently observe anyone who enters their territory, often leaving only psychological effects.

THE ABANDONED EXPERIMENT

The Abandoned Experiment HT_05 was a failed military project aimed at enhancing human strength and endurance through laboratory-engineered transformation. HT_05 was originally Emilia Peterson, a 23-year-old woman abducted by Vladimir Anatoly Zakhaev, a former M.R.I.S. researcher. The experiment stemmed from Incident 922 in 2000, which produced unstable humanoid biological constructs rather than infectious entities.

THE UNBIDDEN HOUSEMATE

The Unbidden Housemate is a rare (distant) human species that closely resembles humans but exhibits predatory, wild behavior and superior physical traits such as greater size, bone density, endurance, and destructive teeth. Ancient civilizations describe them as the Shadowborn Species or the Azodrius, believed to have diverged from humanity long ago and historically hunted humans for survival or dominance.
